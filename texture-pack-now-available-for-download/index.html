Texture pack now available!

> Posted by ein in News

The Crafti.ca texture pack has been released, and is available on our downloads page. A preview is also available demonstrating the pack in a handful of environments. It is currently up-to-date for version 1.4.7 of Minecraft. Preliminary textures for 1.5 are also in the works, so hopefully an update will be available once Jeb finishes mucking about with these snapshots and actually pushes us the upcoming changes. Please keep in mind that because this is a 32x pack, some of the features such as enhanced lava and water animations will not be available unless you first install Optifine.
