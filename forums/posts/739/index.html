I believe that we should have the factions of Hero's and Bandit's added to the whole scheme of decimation because of how we can provide more incite to how we the people of this game should be able to play it how they want it, you wanna be a hero and save others in the wastelands you can be like that, or if your savage bandit looking to kill all survivors you can do this as well. That is why we should introduce the idea of being part of N.P.C factions considering we've area added a bandit mob into the game already. So I was thinking of adding different types of mob styles like Hero N.P.C's and Bandit N.P.C's, so this is what I recommend.

Bandit:
-If you raise your humanity to bandit,you can either choose to join the bandits or become a solo bandit
if you do however decide either path, if you choose to join the bandits, bandit mobs won't shoot you they will actually assist you in killing
other players in the game.
-Another thing when you join the Bandit Faction is when you kill Hero's your humanity level will raise lower than normal since your a bandit making infamy
higher among other bandits around your area allowing you to higher more bandits to join you, there will however will be a number if how many can help you for balance
purposes of course.
-When you become part of the bandits there will be bandit traders walking around the world for you to interact with a walking bandit trader.
-Second to that will be bandit vaults where people can be able to store their items if they so choose. However the safezones will no longer be available but instead you need to go to bandit safezones.


Hero:
-If you decide to however join the Hero's you can be a part of their faction however if you do kill one of them regardless of any reason you will be reverted back to survivor and be blacklisted among the Hero's and they will come after you and will stop once you die.
-When you choose this path same with bandits you can recruit Hero's to join you along your journey throughout decimation if you so desire.
-Along with these Hero's if you companion them will heal you as a bandit will not, whilst fighting they will on contact heal you, when they die they drop medkits or morphine with a droprate of 25%.
-As you join Hero's you will gain more humanity when you kill more zombies and killing bandits will raise it even higher than before. However same as bandits you cannot go back to survivor safezones as it's a lawless place for anyone to come.


Balance:
-Bandits aswell as Hero's you can only have 2 or 3 with you at all times, it's your maximum you can have.
-In factions the rules still apply when your with them,remember.
-The skills you have in-game every time you level up will level up your companions you have with you.
-The safezones for both of the factions will have guards at the entrance as well as the normal safezones so be warry when you walk in there.
