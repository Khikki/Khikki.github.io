Hello there faithful person reading this, my name is MamedTeumessian, and I think the developers could make this mod feel and work a bit better.

First things I think you should add are more uses for crafting, plus branch out for food so that you have empty cans that are specific to what they are before, but can still be used to make Water in a Can or Bottled Water. Overall there should also be more reasons to eat food or drink some water, as there should be two new things that I personally feel would fit in well, which is Heat Stroke/An overall heat system and a hunger disease or infection. The Heat Stroke symptom would slowly decrease your water, and once it reaches to 50% or lower, you start taking a bit of damage. The hunger disease/infection could do the same thing but with hunger. The Heat Stroke symptom could be cured by either drinking some water and decreasing your heat, or you could take a certain type of pill or vaccine to temporarily stop it. The hunger disease wouldn't be related to heat at all, but getting hit by a bullet or zombie could cause it. The only way that you would be able to cure the hunger disease would be by taking a vaccine or pill to cure it.

More things that should be added are nearly all related to vehicles or vehicle bugs. First, you should be able to find some cars out in the cities or maybe on a road that aren't too damaged but require some parts, like an Engine or Battery or some tires, and then you can fuel it up and drive about, one that I could think of is a Sedan that goes a tiny bit faster than an ATV, but requires more fuel. Another part of this Car Update is that you should add a random-generation map for the Singleplayer games that can spawn these, so people can practice looting or something else.

Another thing that I want to see is more melees and a bit of a different melee system. The thing about melee in decimation is that there is so little, and once you get a Makarov or APS, you're set for killing zombies. There should be more common melees and more melees overall so that you don't just straight go to a Makarov or even an SMG. These new melees could be Broken Bottles, Katanas, Swords, Nails, Power Tools, bits of wood, a metal pipe, and even more.

Also, there are a few bugs that I have seen people use. One of these bugs is the ability to go through walls by disconnecting from the game and joining again, then you are in the block to either your left or right. I found this glitch as I was stuck in a hole, so I disconnected and waited for staff. I was near some metal rods and I joined back in the next day and I realized I was in the rods, so I walked out and was free again, only to be shot by a player with an ACR. Also, ATVs sometimes like to just stop and go through walls and stuff in a certain velocity, usually when a server lags.


That is all I have for now, but I will probably post more.
