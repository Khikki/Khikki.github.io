Crafti.ca » texture pack We Build the World Mon, 21 Oct 2013 17:47:09 +0000 en-US hourly 1 http://wordpress.org/?v=3.8 Texture Pack Update to 1.5.1 /texture-pack-update-to-1-5-1/ /texture-pack-update-to-1-5-1/#comments Fri, 29 Mar 2013 16:12:21 +0000 /?p=728 As many of you know, the server was upgraded to 1.5.1 earlier last week, and accordingly the way Minecraft clients handle textures has changed dramatically. I’ve spent the past few days going over the pack and revising it for 1.5.1, and I’m happy to say that I’ve reached a point where I’m happy to provide an up-to-date version for the latest version of Minecraft. This new version of the texture pack no longer requires Optifine, but players who go to the extra trouble of installing it will be treated to CTM support and more attractive sky textures that are included in the pack.

Some blocks have been altered for visual consistency, and animated textures for the following blocks have been added:

  • Redstone Dust
  • Glowstone Dust
  • Glowstone Blocks
  • Torches
  • Gold and Iron bars
  • All varieties of Bucket
  • Emeralds
  • Diamonds
  • All varieties of Ore Blocks
  • Fire Charges
  • Jack-o-lanterns
  • Comparators and Repeaters
  • Activator Rails
  • Command Blocks
  • Clocks and Compasses

As I find time, I’ll look at updating more of the textures, but for now I wanted to ensure that the pack was available for use.
